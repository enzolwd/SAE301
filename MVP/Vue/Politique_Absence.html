Absence Policy

Article 1: Procedure for Justifying Short Absences

1.1. Notification deadline: Any absence, regardless of duration, must be reported to the project supervisor as soon as possible.

1.2. Submission of proof: To officially justify an absence (e.g., medical certificate, administrative summons, etc.), the student has 48 hours (working days) following their return to submit the supporting document to the supervisor.

1.3. Unjustified absence: Any absence not justified according to procedure 1.2 will be counted in the penalty calculation (see Article 3).

Article 2: Management of Long Absences (Exceeding one week)

2.1. Prior notification: For any foreseeable absence lasting longer than one (1) consecutive week, the student is required to inform the project supervisor beforehand, as soon as the date of absence is known.

2.2. Project continuity: This prior notification aims to allow for work reorganization and ensure project continuity. The student remains required to provide official proof according to the procedure in Article 1.

Article 3: Impact of Unjustified Absences on Assessment

Attendance is an essential component of assessment. Duly justified absences (according to Articles 1 and 2) do not result in any penalty. However, unjustified absences will impact the final grade according to the following scale:

3.1. Tolerance: From 1 to 4 unjustified absences (cumulative over the duration of the project), no penalty is applied to the averages.

3.2. Penalty application: Starting from the 5th unjustified absence, a penalty of -0.5 points will be applied to all skill averages evaluated within the project scope.

3.3. Incremental penalty: For each additional unjustified absence beyond the 5th, the penalty is increased by -0.1 points.
